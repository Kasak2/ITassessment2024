Courses

Kavya School offers a variety of courses designed to help students achieve academic excellence and explore their interests. From foundational subjects to specialized topics, our courses are designed to nurture students' intellectual growth and personal development.

Mathematics

Our mathematics courses focus on building strong foundational skills, logical reasoning, and problem-solving abilities. We offer courses for different grade levels, ensuring each student is challenged appropriately.

Science

Our science courses encourage students to explore the world around them through hands-on experiments, projects, and engaging lessons. We offer courses in biology, chemistry, and physics to help students develop critical thinking skills.

Language Arts

Our language arts courses aim to develop students' reading, writing, speaking, and listening skills. From literature analysis to creative writing, students gain an appreciation for language and communication.

Social Studies

Our social studies courses provide students with a deep understanding of history, geography, and civics. Through engaging lessons and projects, students learn about different cultures, societies, and historical events.

Computer Science

Our computer science courses introduce students to the world of technology and programming. From coding basics to advanced software development, students gain valuable skills for the future.
