Facilities

At Kavya School, we are proud to offer state-of-the-art facilities that enhance the learning experience for our students. From modern classrooms to excellent sports amenities, we provide everything our students need to excel both academically and personally.

Modern Classrooms

Our classrooms are equipped with the latest technology to support interactive and engaging lessons.

Science Labs

Our well-equipped science labs offer students the opportunity to conduct experiments and learn through practical application.

Sports Facilities

Our sports facilities include a gymnasium, outdoor fields, and courts for a variety of sports.

Library

Our library is a peaceful place for students to study and explore a wide range of books and resources.

Art Studios

Our art studios provide space for students to express their creativity and hone their artistic skills.

Music Rooms

Our music rooms are designed to support musical education and practice for students of all levels.

We are committed to providing top-quality facilities to support our students' academic, artistic, and physical growth. Visit our school to see these facilities in person and learn more about our commitment to excellence.
